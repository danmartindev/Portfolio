A Little About Me

I love learning new technologies and have been trying to transition more into a full stack developer recently. My favorite part of development is creating a user experience that is fluid, but also makes the user stop and say, “I want to see that again”. Engagement can be improved massively through a good user experience, whether that’s through an animation, a cool presentation, or even a satisfying button click. Developing an experience that makes the user want to interact with it regardless of the subject matter is always a fun challenge for me.
